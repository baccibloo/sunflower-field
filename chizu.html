Playing in the Sunflower Field

The game's locations

Set in a region based in the northern Kanto prefecture, Bokunatsu has players travel back and forth between a set of areas that surround the Sorano household, making for a rather compact area to explore. Despite its seemingly limited extension, however, each of the game’s locations is not only lively and beautifully detailed, but they all convey something related to particular characters, which you can see, for instance, in the lookout place you can often see Moe in, or in Saori’s campsite, or even in the underpass gang’s hideout.
