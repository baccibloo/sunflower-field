Playing in the Sunflower Field

Beetle Sumo Championship

Once Boku has spent a couple of days with his relatives, his summer activities can include exploring the area, watering flowers, fishing, and catching bugs. The latter of these activities offers players a minigame that requires them to interact with a set of characters outside the Sorano family: the underpass gang. A modernized take on the hobby of bug catching and collecting, the game slightly departs from its realistic atmosphere in order to have Boku and his three new friends pit beetles against each other in a makeshift shikona. In addition to the minigame being exciting in itself, with looking for beetles (that you don’t directly control but can still push or cheer on during battles) to outmatch the other kids requiring some strategy and attention to your surroundings, becoming the local beetle sumo champion earns you both the respect of the local gang and access to a secret area that will make exploring areas relevant to late-in-the-game plotlines much easier.
