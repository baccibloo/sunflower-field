Playing in the Sunflower Field

Boku in the eyes of other people

As much as this is Boku’s (and the player’s) vacation to make the most of, the character’s interactions with the rest of the cast undoubtedly make for some of the most touching moments during his time in the countryside. Through the lenses of both aunt Kaoru and Saori’s cameras, the game offers players little souvenirs that show different sides of his personality, from the mischievous (and referential) poses he strikes for Saori’s instant camera, to the more candid pictures Boku’s aunt take of him during different points of the game, these all add little emotional layers to the playable character. As a little bonus, if the player has been constant enough with the flower-watering role that may or may not fall upon them, Boku’s aunt will reward them with some close-ups of the flowers they so diligently took care of.
